Who He Is

Architect. Founder.
Based in Hosur.

Ar. Chittrarasan is the founder and principal architect of Design Intend, a COA-registered architecture and interior design studio based in Hosur, Tamil Nadu. He founded the studio in 2014, after completing his B.Arch from Adhiyaman College of Engineering, Hosur, and working as an Associate Architect at Gensler Bengaluru from 2011 to 2014. He has personally led 85+ projects over 12 years of practice, covering residential homes, farmhouses, villas, and corporate offices across Hosur, Krishnagiri, Bengaluru, and Kerala. Every project at Design Intend is led by Chittrarasan directly.

That is not a marketing claim. It is how the studio is structured. Chittrarasan handles the design decisions, the site visits, and the client conversations at every stage. When something on site does not match the drawings, he is the one who resolves it. When a client has a question three months into construction, they reach him directly. Most of Design Intend's new work comes through referrals from previous clients, which is the clearest evidence this approach actually works.

"Every project starts with a site visit and a long conversation. The brief matters more than the budget. If I do not understand how you live, I cannot design your home."

— Ar. Chittrarasan

How He Thinks

Three Things That
Never Change

There are architecture practices that win awards for photographs. Design Intend is not one of them. The work is designed to be comfortable to live in, to age without constant repair, and to still feel right five years after handover. Three principles have held across every project since 2014.

01

The Site Comes First

Before design begins, we visit the land. Sun orientation, prevailing wind, soil conditions, neighbouring structures, setback constraints — these shape the building more than any style preference. A house placed wrong on a plot will fight its context forever. A house placed right almost designs itself.

02

The Brief Is the Bible

We do not impose a signature style. We design for the people who will live or work in the building. A retired couple building their final home has completely different needs from a family with young children or a company fitting out its first office. The brief tells us what the building needs to do. Everything else follows.

03

Good Enough Is Not Enough

Hosur has contractors who will build you something that looks fine at possession and starts showing problems two monsoons later. Design Intend uses only structural engineers and execution partners whose work we would stand behind. Our drawings are detailed enough that there is no room for improvisation on site.

How It Works

Direct Access.
Every Stage.

When you work with Design Intend, you work with Chittrarasan. Not a project coordinator. Not a junior architect who has been briefed. Chittrarasan conducts the initial site visit, develops the design, manages the drawings, and is reachable directly during construction.

This is not common. In most architecture practices of any size, the principal meets you at the beginning and again at handover. In between, a team manages the project. Design Intend is structured differently by deliberate choice. The quality of the work depends on one person's attention across every stage. That is only possible if that person is actually there.

Most of Design Intend's new projects come from referrals. Clients who built with the studio recommend it to siblings, friends, colleagues, and employers. That pattern tells you more about what working with Chittrarasan is like than anything this page can say. If you want references, ask — and we will connect you with past clients directly.

"We are probably not the right studio for the cheapest possible drawings with no follow-up. If that is what you need, there are faster and cheaper options in Hosur. We are for clients who want to build something once and not regret it."

— Ar. Chittrarasan
