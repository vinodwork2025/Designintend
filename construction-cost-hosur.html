Common Questions

What factors increase the construction cost above the base rate?+
  • Rocky or soft soil — requires deeper, costlier foundations
  • Irregular or sloped plot — more material and design complexity
  • Basement — adds ₹1,200–₹1,500/sq.ft for the basement area
  • Design complexity — curves, cantilevers, large glass facades
  • Mid-project design changes — always quoted before proceeding
How long does construction take in Hosur?+

For a typical home: design + approval takes 2–3 months. Construction: 10–12 months (under 1,200 sq.ft), 12–15 months (1,200–2,000 sq.ft), 15–20 months (above 2,000 sq.ft). Design Intend provides a written construction schedule before work begins — no vague timelines.

Is the rate on built-up area or carpet area?+

Our rates are quoted on built-up area (BUA) — the total constructed area including walls, staircase, utility spaces, and balconies. Carpet area (usable space inside walls) is approximately 70–75% of BUA. A 2,000 sq.ft BUA home has roughly 1,400–1,500 sq.ft of usable space.

Do you offer a fixed-price contract?+

Yes. Design Intend works on a fixed-rate contract tied to the agreed scope of work. Payments follow construction milestones: 10% on signing, 20% on foundation, and so on until 10% on final handover. You only pay when you can see the completed work — no large advances.
