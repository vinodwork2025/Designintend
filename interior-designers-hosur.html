What We Do

More Than Just Decoration

A lot of people think interior design is about picking curtains and paint colors. It is much more than that. Good interior design starts with how your space is planned — where the furniture goes, how light moves through the room, whether the kitchen layout actually makes sense for daily use.

At Design Intend, we are interior designers in Hosur who work on the whole picture. We look at how you live, what you need from each room, and how to make the space feel right without wasting money on things that do not matter.

We have been doing this since 2014. Over 180 projects later, we know what works in Hosur homes and offices, and we know what tends to go wrong when people skip proper planning.

The Problem

Why Interior Projects Go Wrong

Most people who have had a bad interior experience will tell you the same things. The work took way longer than promised. The final cost was higher than the quote. The contractor disappeared for days at a time. And the end result looked nothing like what was shown in the catalog photos.

Finding good interior designers in Hosur is not as easy as it should be. Many decorators work with limited subcontractors, have no formal design process, and use whatever materials are cheapest at the time. You only find out when the laminate starts peeling or the wardrobe door warps after the first monsoon.

The issues we hear most often:

  • Quotations that keep climbing after work starts
  • No proper drawings or 3D preview before execution
  • Workmanship problems that show up within the first year
  • Poor material choices that look good on day one but age badly
  • Delayed timelines with no clear reason or accountability
  • No single point of contact — you end up managing multiple people yourself

We hear these complaints regularly from clients who come to us after a first bad experience somewhere else. It does not have to be this way.

Interior Design Services

What Our Home Interiors Cover

We offer complete interior design services in Hosur for residential and commercial spaces. Here is what we handle:

Living Room Interiors

Space planning, TV unit design, false ceiling with lighting, wall paneling or wallpaper, sofa and furniture selection, and curtain/blind coordination. We make sure the room flows well and looks good both in daylight and in the evening.

Bedroom Interiors

Custom wardrobes with internal organization, bed design with headboard, study unit if needed, and coordinated lighting. We design master bedrooms, kids rooms, and guest rooms with specific needs in mind for each.

Kitchen Interiors

Modular kitchen design with proper workflow planning, choice of shutters (laminate, acrylic, PU, or membrane), countertop selection, loft units, chimney placement, and appliance integration. A well-designed kitchen genuinely makes daily cooking easier.

Bathroom Interiors

Tile selection and layout, vanity unit design, shower enclosure, mirror with backlit frame, storage solutions, and fixture recommendations. We turn bathrooms from purely functional spaces into rooms you actually like using.

Office and Commercial Interiors

Workstation layouts, cabin design, reception area, conference room, pantry, and branded wall treatments. We have done offices ranging from 500 sq ft startups to 4,000 sq ft corporate floors in and around Hosur.

Full Home Interior Packages

If you want everything handled at once, our home interior packages for 2BHK and 3BHK cover all rooms together. This keeps the design consistent across the home and usually saves money compared to doing rooms one by one.

Our Process

From First Visit to Final Handover

Here is exactly how a project with our interior designers in Hosur moves forward:

1

Site Visit and Briefing

We visit your home or office, take measurements, understand how you use the space, discuss your taste, and note any specific requirements. This takes about an hour and is completely free.

2

Design Concept

Based on your brief, we put together a design direction — overall look and feel, color palette, material theme, and furniture style. We present this for your feedback before going deeper.

3

3D Visualization

We create room-by-room 3D renders so you can see exactly what your finished home will look like. This is the stage where you review and approve every detail — cabinets, finishes, lighting positions, tile patterns.

4

Fixed Quote and Timeline

Once the design is approved, we give you a detailed quote with itemized costs and a clear timeline. This quote is fixed. The timeline is realistic, not optimistic.

5

Execution

Our in-house team handles civil work, carpentry, electrical, painting, and final fit-out. A dedicated site supervisor monitors progress daily and you get regular updates.

6

Handover

We do a walkthrough with you before we consider the job done. Any punch list items are sorted before final payment. After handover, we remain contactable for post-project support.

How long does interior work take for a 2BHK in Hosur? +

A full 2BHK interior typically takes 35 to 50 days with our team, provided the design is approved and materials are selected before work starts. We share a week-by-week schedule before we begin so you know what to expect at every stage.

Do you provide 3D designs before starting work? +

Yes, always. Every project gets detailed 3D renders before any physical work begins. You see each room from multiple angles, with actual materials and colors applied. We do not start on site until you have reviewed and approved the 3D design.

Can I choose my own materials and brands? +

Absolutely. We present options across different price points — budget, mid-range, and premium. You pick what fits your taste and budget. We never push a specific brand or material because we have a tie-up with someone. We do, however, advise against materials that we know will not last.

Do you handle both new homes and renovation projects? +

Yes, both. New home interiors and renovation projects each have their own challenges and we are experienced with both. For renovations, we assess what can stay, what needs to be redone, and how to minimize disruption to your day-to-day life during the work.
