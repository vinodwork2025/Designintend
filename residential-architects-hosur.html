What Goes Wrong

Why Many Hosur Homes
Do Not Turn Out Right

Many people in Hosur build their homes without hiring a proper residential architect. They use a draftsman for the approval drawing and a contractor who builds from experience rather than from a proper set of working drawings. This approach is cheaper upfront. It is almost always more expensive by the time the house is done.

Floor plan not suited to the plot or the family

A good residential architect in Hosur designs the floor plan around how your family actually lives. How many people. Who needs privacy. Whether the kitchen needs to be close to the car entrance for groceries. Whether there is a family member who needs a ground floor bedroom. A copy-paste plan from another project does not account for any of this.

Vastu ignored or badly applied

Many families in Hosur want their home to follow Vastu principles. A good residential architect can incorporate Vastu into the design without creating a floor plan that does not work functionally. Bad Vastu application results in rooms that face the wrong direction, awkward staircase positions, and bathrooms in locations that create practical problems. We design Vastu-compliant homes that are also genuinely functional.

No 3D preview before construction

A floor plan is hard for most people to read. You can approve a drawing and not realise the staircase takes up half your first-floor landing, or that the master bedroom window faces directly into the neighbour's kitchen. A 3D elevation lets you see exactly what the building will look like before any concrete is poured. We prepare these for every project.

Approval drawings that do not match what gets built

A common problem in Hosur is that the approval drawing is prepared for the authority and the actual construction follows a different plan that the contractor improvises. This creates legal issues later when you try to get a completion certificate or sell the property. We prepare approval drawings that match the actual construction plan.

What We Provide

Residential Architecture
Services in Hosur

Floor Plan Design

Custom floor plans designed around your plot dimensions, setback requirements, and family needs. We prepare multiple options before settling on the plan you approve. Each option is drawn to scale and explained clearly in plain language — not just handed over as a drawing you are expected to interpret yourself.

3D Elevation and Exterior Design

3D views of your home's exterior showing the front, back, and side elevations with actual material finishes. You see the roof design, the window positions, the car porch, compound wall, and garden area. This is how you know your home will look the way you imagined before construction starts.

Structural and Working Drawings

Complete working drawing sets including structural drawings prepared with a licensed structural engineer, plumbing schematics, electrical layout, and construction details. These are the drawings your contractor needs to build the home correctly. Without a proper working drawing set, the contractor guesses on important structural decisions.

Approval Drawings and Submissions

Building plan drawings in the format required by the relevant local authority in Hosur. Submission and follow-up handled by our team. We know the specific requirements of the Hosur local bodies and prepare documents that are accepted on the first submission in most cases.

Construction Supervision

Site visits by our architects throughout construction to verify that the building is going up according to the approved drawings and specifications. We check critical milestones: foundation depth, column spacing, beam dimensions, plumb and level checks, and finishing quality before each stage is covered up.

What does a residential architect in Hosur do? +

A residential architect in Hosur designs your home from scratch or adapts an existing design to your plot and requirements. The work includes floor plans, elevations, structural drawings, plumbing and electrical layouts, and approval documents. A good residential architect also visits the site during construction to ensure the building matches the approved drawings.

How much does it cost to hire a residential architect in Hosur? +

Residential architect fees in Hosur range from Rs 30 to 80 per sq ft for design and drawings. For a 1,500 sq ft home, the fee is typically Rs 75,000 to Rs 1.2 lakh. Do residential architects in Hosur handle building plan approvals? +

Yes. We prepare approval drawings in the format required by the Hosur local body, DTCP, or CMDA depending on your plot location. We submit the application and follow up on your behalf. The approval timeline is typically 30 to 90 days depending on the authority.
