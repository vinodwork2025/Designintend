Why Interior Design Matters in Bagalur

Small Homes. Smart Planning.

Bagalur homes are typically built on 30x40 or 30x50 plots. The rooms are not large. A 2BHK here is usually 900 to 1,100 sq ft. That means every square foot of interior space has to work harder than in a bigger home.

The most common interior mistakes we see in Bagalur homes are not about style. They are about planning. The carpenter made a wardrobe that blocks the window. The kitchen has no space for the refrigerator after the modular units went in. The false ceiling hides a plumbing pipe and now leaks when the pipe is serviced. These are not design problems. They are coordination problems.

Interior designers in Bagalur who plan everything on paper before fabrication starts prevent these issues. We design all rooms together, coordinating the electrical points, plumbing, and structure before a single board is cut.

Who our Bagalur interior clients are

Most of our Bagalur interior clients are families who just built or bought their first home. Many work at SIPCOT or nearby factories. They have a clear budget, they know what they want in general terms, and they want interiors that last without constant repair or replacement. They are not looking for magazine spreads. They want their home to be practical, clean, and comfortable.

We respect that. Our design approach in Bagalur focuses on storage, material durability, and kitchen functionality. Everything else follows from those three priorities.

What We Design

Interior Design Services in Bagalur

We handle the complete interior of your Bagalur home or individual rooms depending on your budget and priorities. Here is what each part of the service covers.

Modular Kitchen

Full design with L-shape or straight layout, upper and lower cabinets, drawer units, chimney housing, and countertop. BWR plywood carcass with laminate or acrylic shutters. Soft-close hinges standard.

Wardrobes

Floor-to-ceiling sliding or hinged wardrobes for each bedroom. Internal fittings: hanging rod, shelves, drawer unit, and a small mirror. Designed to use wall height fully so no dead space above.

Living Room

TV unit with storage, display shelves, and cable management. Optional: console table or crockery unit. False ceiling with recessed lighting and a fan provision. We keep it clean and functional.

False Ceiling and Lighting

Gypsum or PVC false ceiling with recessed LED lighting plan. We design the lighting zones before any wiring is done so the electrician knows where the points go. No retrofitting.

Bathroom and Utility

Vanity unit, mirror with frame or backlit panel, towel ring, and accessories. Utility area: washing machine ledge, storage shelf, and water heater housing.

Foyer and Entrance

Shoe rack with seat, coat hooks, and a small storage unit. If the foyer is narrow, we use a wall-mounted fold-down bench. First impression sets the tone for the whole house.

How It Works

From Empty Rooms to Finished Home

Step 1: Site measurement and brief

We visit your Bagalur home, measure every room, check existing electrical points and plumbing, and discuss what you want room by room. You tell us your priorities, your preferred materials, and your budget range. This takes about 90 minutes on site.

Step 2: Design and 3D visualisation

We prepare a floor plan and 3D view for each room so you can see exactly how the furniture and storage will look. You review and give feedback. Changes at this stage cost nothing. We proceed to fabrication only after you approve the design.

Step 3: Material selection

We show you sample boards for plywood finish, laminate colours, shutter material, countertop, and hardware. You choose from a curated set that works for your budget. No unpleasant surprises when materials arrive on site.

Step 4: Fabrication and installation

Kitchen and wardrobe units are factory fabricated and installed on site. False ceiling, painting, and tile work is done by our civil team. Everything runs in a planned sequence so trades do not block each other. A site supervisor is present daily.

Step 5: Handover

Final walkthrough, snag list, and cleaning. One year warranty on all carpentry work. Any hardware failure within the warranty period is replaced at no cost.

Questions About Interior Design in Bagalur

How much does interior design cost in Bagalur? +

A full 2BHK interior package in Bagalur costs Rs 6 to Rs 10 lakh, covering kitchen, wardrobes, TV unit, false ceiling, and foyer. A 3BHK package runs Rs 10 to Rs 16 lakh. Individual items like just the kitchen run Rs 1.8 to Rs 2.8 lakh. You can phase the work over time if budget is a concern.

What should be included in a 2BHK interior package in Bagalur? +

A complete 2BHK interior package should include a modular kitchen, a wardrobe in each bedroom, a TV unit in the living room, foyer storage, false ceiling with lighting in the living room and kitchen, and bathroom accessories. Optional additions are a study corner, loft storage, or a crockery unit in the dining area.

Is a modular kitchen worth it for a Bagalur home? +

Yes. A modular kitchen uses factory-finished materials that do not warp, is easier to clean, lasts 15 to 20 years with normal use, and can be designed to fit kitchens as small as 6x8 ft. For a compact Bagalur kitchen, a modular setup gives you far better storage and durability than site-built carpentry at a comparable or lower cost.

How long does interior work take after construction in Bagalur? +

Interior work for a 2BHK home takes 6 to 8 weeks from design approval to completion. A 3BHK takes 8 to 12 weeks. The design phase takes 1 to 2 weeks before any fabrication starts. Total timeline from first meeting to handover is typically 10 to 14 weeks for a 2BHK.

Can I do interior work in phases and not all at once? +

Yes. We design everything together first so the phases match. You can start with the kitchen and master bedroom, move in, then do the second bedroom and living room three to six months later. The design is already done so the second phase slots in without any redesign cost.

Do you handle interior design for homes built by other contractors in Bagalur? +

Yes. We do interior projects for homes we did not build. We do a site visit to check electrical points, slab openings, and existing conditions, then design around what is in place. The process is the same as for homes we built. Call us with your address in Bagalur and we will schedule a visit.
