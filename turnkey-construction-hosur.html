What It Means

What Is Turnkey Construction?

Turnkey construction means you deal with one company for everything. You sign one contract, pay milestone-based installments to one team, and at the end of the project you receive a fully finished home. No separate agreements with an architect, a civil contractor, and an interior designer. No overlap, no confusion, no blame game.

The name comes from the idea that you just "turn the key" and walk in. Everything is done: walls, flooring, kitchen, wardrobes, painting, electrical fittings, plumbing fixtures. You don't arrive to a bare concrete box.

For most people in Hosur who are building for the first time, this is the most practical way to build. You likely have a day job, a family, and limited bandwidth to chase three or four vendors every week. Turnkey construction in Hosur solves exactly that problem.

Why managing multiple vendors gets complicated

The common approach is to hire an architect for drawings, a civil contractor for construction, and then bring in an interior designer later. On paper it sounds fine. In practice, these three often don't talk to each other.

The architect draws a kitchen layout with a 10-foot counter. The contractor builds a 9-foot counter because materials ran short. The interior person arrives and says the modular kitchen unit won't fit. Now you're calling all three to figure out who is responsible, and everyone points at someone else.

These coordination gaps cause cost overruns, delays, and rework. We've seen projects that were supposed to cost Rs 45 lakh end up at Rs 58 lakh simply because of poor coordination between separate vendors.

What affects the final cost

  • Number of floors: each additional floor adds Rs 2,500-3,000/sq ft for the new area
  • Plot shape: irregular or corner plots need more structural work
  • Basement or stilt floor: adds Rs 1,800-2,200/sq ft
  • Rocky soil in some Hosur areas: excavation costs vary based on rock layer depth
  • Kitchen and wardrobe finish: laminate vs lacquered vs acrylic makes a significant price difference
  • Bathroom fittings: Rs 15,000 vs Rs 60,000 per bathroom depending on brand choice

Common Questions About Turnkey Construction

What does turnkey construction mean? +

Turnkey construction means you sign one contract with one company and they handle everything from design to handover. Architectural drawings, government approvals, civil construction, electrical, plumbing, interior design, modular kitchen, wardrobes, painting — all done by one team. You receive a fully finished, move-in ready home at the end.

Is turnkey construction more expensive than hiring separate vendors? +

The per square foot rate is higher, but the total project cost is usually similar or lower. When you hire separate vendors, you typically pay Rs 2-6 lakh in rework costs from coordination errors, plus delays that push up labour costs. Turnkey construction gives you a fixed total so there are no surprises. Most of our clients save 15-25% compared to what they had originally budgeted for the three-vendor approach.

What is included in a turnkey construction package in Hosur? +

Our turnkey package covers architectural design, structural drawings, DTCP or panchayat building approval, soil testing, foundation to roof civil construction, electrical wiring and fittings, CPVC plumbing, interior design for all rooms, modular kitchen, built-in wardrobes, false ceiling, flooring, painting, and final handover with a 1-year defect warranty. Land cost, approval fees, borewell, and compound wall are separate.

How long does a turnkey home project take? +

A 1,500-2,000 sq ft home typically takes 14-18 months from the first meeting to handover. Design takes 6-8 weeks. Government approvals take 6-10 weeks depending on your local body. Civil construction takes 10-14 months. Interior work runs parallel in the last 3-4 months of construction, so there is no extra waiting time for that phase.

What happens if I want changes during construction? +

Minor changes like switching a tile pattern or changing a paint colour have no extra cost. For structural or layout changes, we prepare a change order with the revised cost and timeline before any work is done. You approve it, then we proceed. We never make a change and surprise you with a bill later.

Yes. We handle turnkey construction projects in Hosur, Attibele, Bagalur, Denkanikottai, Krishnagiri, and surrounding areas within about 60 km. Our site supervision schedule is the same regardless of location. Call us and tell us where your plot is — we'll let you know if we can take it on.
