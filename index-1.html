About the Studio

The Design Intend
Philosophy

Design Intend was founded in 2009 with one conviction that has never wavered: great architecture is not the construction of buildings — it is the creation of environments that shape how people live, work, and feel.

Every project starts with deep listening. We immerse ourselves in a site, a brief, and a life before a single line is drawn. The result is architecture that feels inevitable — as if the building could not possibly have taken any other form.

"We don't design buildings.
We design the lives that happen inside them."
— Design Intend, Founding Principle
School of Architecture & Planning New Delhi, B.Arch, 2004
IIA Registered Practice Indian Institute of Architects
Architecture + Design Award Listed 2021, 2023 & 2024

How We Work

Our
Process

01

Discovery

An in-depth conversation about your vision, site, budget, and timeline. We study the land, the light, and the life you intend to live before imagining anything.

02

Concept

A complete design concept — massing models, spatial narratives, material palettes, and 3D visualisations that make the architecture real before a brick is laid.

03

Refinement

Collaborative iteration until every detail is exactly right — not approximately right. Working drawings, specifications, and approvals handled in full.

04

Execution

Full site supervision, contractor coordination, quality assurance, and handover. We remain present and accountable until your space is exactly as designed.
