Construction Quality

Materials We Use — No Compromise

Every client deserves to know what is going into their building. We are transparent about this. These are the specifications we use across our standard and premium packages:

Structure

RCC framed structure. Ultratech or Ramco OPC 53 grade cement. TATA Tiscon or JSW Neosteel TMT bars (Fe500D).

Masonry

AAC blocks or solid concrete blocks. Plastering with river sand and cement mortar. Double-coat internal plaster, external weather coat.

Flooring

Vitrified tiles (600x600mm or larger) for living and bedrooms. Anti-skid ceramic tiles for bathrooms and kitchen. Granite for kitchen countertops.

Electrical

Concealed wiring with Anchor/Legrand modular switches. MCB distribution board with ELCB. ISI-certified cables throughout.

Plumbing

Astral or Finolex CPVC pipes. ISI-certified CP fittings. Separate lines for overhead and underground tank. EWC and washbasins from reputed brands.

Doors and Windows

Teak wood or engineered wood for main door. Internal doors with block board shutters and quality hardware. uPVC windows with mosquito mesh.

We maintain a materials register for every project. You can verify what was used, when it was delivered, and which batch it came from. This level of documentation is rare among builders in Hosur and it is something we have done since day one.

How We Build

The Construction Process Step by Step

1

Site Survey and Design

We visit your plot, take soil and site conditions into account, and develop an architectural design that fits your brief. You see the floor plan and 3D exterior before you sign anything.

2

Approval and Permits

We prepare and submit building plan drawings to the relevant authority — DTCP Hosur or local panchayat office depending on your plot location. We track the approval and handle any queries.

3

Foundation and Substructure

Soil testing, foundation design, and construction of the foundation and plinth. All concrete work is cube-tested and supervised by our site engineer. You receive a foundation completion report.

4

Structural Frame

Columns, beams, slabs, and staircase. Steel bar bending schedules are followed strictly. Concrete mix design is maintained and slump is tested on every pour. No shortcuts in the structure.

5

Masonry, Plastering, and MEP

Block work, internal and external plastering, concealed electrical conduit and wiring, plumbing lines, and waterproofing in wet areas. All done in sequence, not rushed together.

6

Finishing and Handover

Flooring, painting, door and window installation, fixtures, kitchen slab, bathroom fittings, and final electrical connections. We do a formal walkthrough with you before handover and address every item on the snag list.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost per sq ft for construction in Hosur? +

Our current rates are Rs 1,950 per sq ft for standard finish, Rs 2,050 per sq ft for premium finish, and Rs 2,300 per sq ft for luxury finish. These are all-inclusive rates covering structure, masonry, plastering, flooring, painting, doors, windows, electrical, and plumbing. How do you ensure the construction price doesn't change mid-project? +

We use a fixed price contract based on a detailed scope document that specifies materials, brands, and construction methods. The price in that contract is the price you pay unless you decide to change something. If you want to add or upgrade anything, we give you a separate written quote for that variation. Price never changes without your written approval.

What materials do you use for home construction? +

We use Ultratech or Ramco cement, TATA or JSW TMT steel bars, Anchor or Legrand electrical fittings, and Astral or Finolex CPVC pipes. Flooring is vitrified tiles for living areas and anti-skid ceramic for bathrooms. All materials are ISI-certified. We maintain a materials register for every project that you can check at any time.

How long does it take to build a house in Hosur? +

A standard home between 1,500 and 2,000 sq ft typically takes 10 to 14 months from foundation start to handover. Do you construct on customer-owned plots? +

Yes, and this is how most of our projects work. You own the plot in Hosur or nearby, and we handle architectural design, building approvals, and full construction. We do not require you to buy land from us or go through a developer. Your land, our construction expertise.

What happens if there are delays during construction? +

Our contract includes a timeline with stage milestones. If we fall behind schedule due to our own execution, we are responsible for it and will recover the time wherever possible. We do not spring deadline surprises on clients at the end of a project.
