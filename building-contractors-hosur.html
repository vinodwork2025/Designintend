The Problem

Why Construction Projects
Go Over Budget

Most bad building contractor experiences in Hosur follow a pattern that is very predictable once you know what to look for.

Low initial quote, high final bill

A contractor wins the project with a low quote. Once the foundation is laid and you are committed, items start getting added. Extra charges for electrical conduits. Extra charges for window frames not included in the original scope. Extra charges for soil disposal. A proper quote from a reliable building contractor in Hosur covers all of these upfront. Nothing gets added unless you change the scope yourself.

No architect on site regularly

Many contractors in Hosur are pure civil contractors. They build what is in the drawing but do not have the design capability to resolve issues that come up during construction. When the drawing does not account for a beam position, or when the window placement creates a problem with the staircase, a contractor without design support either guesses or calls you to decide. Both options cost money and time.

Labour turnover on site

One of the most common problems with small contractors in Hosur is that the labour team changes mid-project. The people who did the foundation are not the same people who do the brickwork, and the brickwork team is not the same as the plastering team. Quality control disappears when there is no consistent workforce and no supervisor who knows the history of every decision made on the site.

No proper drawings before work starts

Building without proper working drawings creates expensive corrections later. A column in the wrong position. A bathroom that does not have enough fall for drainage. A door that swings into another door when both are open. These things are easy to catch on paper and very expensive to fix in concrete.
