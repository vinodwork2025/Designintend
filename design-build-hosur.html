What is design and build?

One Company for Design and Construction

Most people in Hosur who build a home go through the same process: hire an architect, then separately find a contractor, and then maybe a third company for interiors. Each of these parties works independently. When problems show up — and they always do — nobody wants to take responsibility.

Design and build in Hosur works differently. One company designs your home and builds it. The architect who drew the plan is on the same team as the engineer who pours the foundation. There is one contract, one timeline, and one phone number to call.

Design Intend has been doing design and build in Hosur since 2014. Our principal architect, Ar. Chittrarasan, has led over 180 projects this way. It is the only model we use — because we believe that if you design something, you should be accountable for building it right.

The problem with the traditional model

Why Separate Architect + Contractor Causes Problems

The traditional approach sounds logical: get the best architect, then get the best contractor. But in practice, it creates a triangle of conflict between you, your designer, and your builder.

The architect says the drawing is correct. The contractor says the drawing is impractical. You are stuck in the middle, paying for delays, rework, and arguments that have nothing to do with your dream home.

Common problems in the traditional approach

  • Design errors discovered only after construction begins — costly to fix
  • Contractor quotes a low number then adds variations charges every month
  • Architect supervision stops after drawings are delivered — no site oversight
  • Interior work handed to a third company who has never seen the structural drawings
  • No one person accountable for the final quality — everyone points at someone else
  • Timeline slips because design and build decisions are made by different teams with different priorities

If you have the time and energy to manage three separate vendors, traditional works. Most people don't — especially NRI clients or those living in Bangalore who are building in Hosur.

How it works

The Design-Build Process at Design Intend

Here is how a typical design and build project in Hosur moves from your first call to keys in hand:

1

Discovery Call

We understand your project — plot size, family size, budget range, and timeline. This is a 20-minute call, no commitment.

2

Site Visit

We visit your plot in Hosur or the surrounding area. We check orientation, access, soil type, and utility connections. This visit is paid (Rs 2,500), adjusted against the project fee if you sign with us.

3

Design Brief

You fill a structured brief — room requirements, style preferences, must-haves, and budget limits. This becomes the foundation for the design, not guesswork.

4

Concept Presentation

We present floor plans, elevations, and 3D views. You give feedback, we revise. Most projects need 2 revision rounds at this stage.

5

Design Approval + BOQ

Once you approve the final design, we prepare a detailed Bill of Quantities. Every material, every brand, every rate — listed before you sign anything.

6

Contract + Commencement

We sign a fixed-price contract with milestone payments tied to site progress. Construction begins after DTCP approval (or simultaneously if self-certified plot conditions apply).

7

Milestone Updates

You receive photo and video updates at every major milestone — footing, plinth, roof slab, plastering, finishes, and handover. No surprises, no silence.

8

Final Handover

We do a joint walkthrough before handing over keys. Every snag is resolved, every fitting is checked. You move in to a complete, finished home.

Common Questions

What is design and build and how is it different from hiring separate vendors? +

In a traditional setup, you hire an architect for design, then separately find a contractor to build, and often a third firm for interiors. These three parties don't work for each other, so when problems come up, there's no single person accountable. In a design and build arrangement, one firm handles the entire project — design, approvals, construction, and interiors — under a single contract. You deal with one team, one timeline, and one point of accountability.

Is design and build more expensive than the traditional approach? +

The upfront quote may look higher, but total project cost usually works out lower. When the same team designs and builds, there are fewer design errors that cost money to fix during construction. You also save on coordination delays. Hidden costs in the traditional model — like rework when the contractor says the drawing doesn't match site conditions — simply don't happen when one team owns both the drawing and the build.

Who is the single point of contact in a design-build project? +

At Design Intend, your project is managed by our principal architect, Ar. Chittrarasan, supported by a dedicated project manager. You get one phone number, one WhatsApp, one team. There is no passing blame between designer and contractor — we own both sides of the project.

What if I already have a design — can you just build it? +

Yes, we can work with existing designs. We will review the drawings, do a constructability check, prepare the BOQ, and handle the construction. If the design needs modifications for local site conditions or approval compliance, we will flag those upfront. This is called a build-only engagement and we handle these regularly.

How transparent is the pricing in a design-build contract? +

We provide a detailed Bill of Quantities before signing any contract. Every line item is listed — materials, brand specifications, labor rates, and finish costs. You know exactly what you are paying for. Milestone payments are tied to physical progress on site, not arbitrary dates. No variation charges unless you request design changes mid-project.

Do you do design-build projects outside Hosur? +

Yes. We serve Hosur and surrounding areas including Attibele, Bagalur, Denkanikottai, and Krishnagiri. Most projects are within 50 km of our Hosur office. For projects beyond that range, we assess on a case-by-case basis. WhatsApp or call us with your project location and we will give you a straightforward answer.
