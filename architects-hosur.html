Why Architecture Matters

Most Construction Problems Start at the Design Stage

When someone builds a home and runs into problems — walls cracking, rooms that feel too small, bad ventilation, drainage issues, or a kitchen that does not work properly — most of those problems can be traced back to decisions that were made, or not made, before construction started.

Architects in Hosur who do this job properly prevent most of these problems. A good house plan is not just a sketch showing where the rooms go. It accounts for sunlight direction, wind flow, soil conditions, your actual daily routine, future needs, and the site's specific constraints.

At Design Intend, Principal Architect Ar. Chittrarasan leads every project personally. We have designed over 180 buildings in and around Hosur since 2014 and we take architectural quality seriously on every project, regardless of size.

What Goes Wrong

Common Problems With Bad Architecture

People often underestimate what they are getting into when they hire an architect who does the bare minimum — just approval drawings with no real design thinking. Here is what tends to happen:

  • Approval drawings that do not match site conditions, leading to problems mid-construction
  • Floor plans that waste space or create awkward circulation through the home
  • No coordination between architecture and structure, resulting in columns in the wrong places
  • Missing details that the contractor has to improvise on-site — usually badly
  • No support during construction, so design intent gets lost in translation
  • Cost overruns because the design was not thought through before work started

The fix is straightforward: work with architects in Hosur who actually design your building, not just stamp drawings. That is what we do.

How We Work

From Site Visit to Approved Drawings

1

Site Visit and Brief

We visit your plot in Hosur, measure the site, check the orientation, note any constraints, and spend time understanding what you want from the building. This is where we ask the questions most people do not think to ask — like how the morning sun will hit your main bedroom, or whether you want a car porch or basement parking.

2

Concept Design

We develop the initial concept — floor plan options, the look and feel of the building, key design decisions. We present these to you and take your feedback. At this stage everything is easy to change.

3

Detailed Design and 3D

Once the concept is agreed upon, we develop detailed floor plans and create 3D exterior and interior views so you can see what the building will actually look like before we prepare construction drawings.

4

Approval Drawings

We prepare all the documents needed for DTCP building plan approval in Hosur and submit the application on your behalf. We follow up and handle any queries from the authority.

5

Construction Drawings

Full working drawings including structural, plumbing, electrical layouts, and finishing schedules — everything the construction team needs to build correctly without guesswork.

6

Construction Supervision

Regular site visits during construction to verify quality and conformance to design. We are available for queries and decisions throughout the build.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does an architect do for a home construction project in Hosur? +

An architect in Hosur designs your building, prepares all drawings needed for construction, and supports the building permit application process. A good architect also coordinates with the structural engineer, prepares detailed working drawings so contractors have clear instructions, and visits site during construction to verify quality. The result is a building that is safe, well-designed, and built as intended.

How much do architects charge in Hosur? +

Our architectural fees are typically 3% to 5% of the construction cost for full services. For a home with a Rs 80 lakh construction budget, that works out to roughly Rs 2.4 lakh to Rs 4 lakh. For smaller scopes — say, just a house plan and approval drawings — we can quote separately. We discuss the scope and fee clearly before any engagement begins.

Do you help with building approvals in Hosur? +

Yes, approval support is a core part of what we do. We prepare all documents for DTCP building plan approval in Hosur, handle submission, and manage any queries from the authority. Having done this many times in Hosur specifically means we know the requirements and can avoid the common mistakes that cause delays.

Can I build without an architect in Hosur? +

For any formal building plan approval in Hosur, you need drawings signed by a licensed architect.
